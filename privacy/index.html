Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 16, 2026

The short version: 141 venn-U collects only what's necessary to run the platform — your account info, the reviews you submit, and photos you upload. We don't sell your data. Ever.

Who We Are

141 venn-U ("we," "us," or "our") is a stadium and venue review platform located at 141vennu.com. We provide a community-driven scoring system for sports venues using the 1-4-1 method.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@141vennu.com

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the platform:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo when you create an account or sign in with Google
  • Profile information — your display name, username, bio, and scout level you choose to share
  • Review content — venue names, scores, written reviews, and photos you submit
  • Venue passport data — venues you've visited, stamps earned, and badges unlocked
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and interactions within the platform

Google Sign-In

When you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your:

  • Name and profile picture from your Google account
  • Email address associated with your Google account

We use this solely to create and manage your 141 venn-U account. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, Gmail, or any other Google services. Your Google password is never shared with us.

Google's handling of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your reviews, scores, and venue passport to the community
  • Track your scout level and badge progression
  • Improve the platform and fix technical issues
  • Respond to your questions or support requests
  • Send important platform updates (not marketing spam)

Information We Share

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your public profile information (display name, reviews, scores, passport stamps) is visible to other users of the platform — that's the nature of a review community. You control your username and bio.

We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider who stores your account data securely
  • Google — only for OAuth authentication if you choose to sign in with Google
  • Law enforcement — only if required by law or to protect the safety of our users

Photos You Upload

Photos you attach to reviews are stored securely and displayed publicly as part of your review. You retain ownership of photos you upload. By uploading a photo you grant 141 venn-U a license to display it on the platform.

You can request deletion of your photos at any time by contacting us.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, which provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We use HTTPS for all connections to the platform.

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct — update your profile information at any time in your account settings
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request an export of your review data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@141vennu.com.

Cookies & Local Storage

141 venn-U uses cookies and browser local storage solely to maintain your login session. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. We do not run ads.

Children's Privacy

141 venn-U is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about privacy?

Email us at privacy@141vennu.com and we'll respond within 48 hours.
